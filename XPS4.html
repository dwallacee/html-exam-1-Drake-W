The first XPS laptop is released in 2007, the Dell XPS M1330

Source: NotebookCheck. A history of Dell XPS laptops from 2007 to present. 2019. notebookcheck.net (https://www.notebookcheck.net/A-history-of-Dell-XPS-laptops-from-2007-to-present.411002.0.html)

The Dell XPS M1330 proved stylish and powerful for its time. Powering the XPS M1330 was an Intel Core 2 Duo T7300 dual-core CPU, 2 GB of DDR2 RAM, and an NVIDIA GeForce 8400M GS GPU with 128 MB VRAM. Dell offered the M1330 with a 13.3-inch 1280x800 UltraSharp panel that was vibrant and glossy.
