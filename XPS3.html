Dell revamps the XPS line to compete with Alienware, eventually buys alienware in 2006

Source: Wikipedia. Dell XPS. 2023. wikipedia.org (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dell_XPS#XPS_10)

In 2006, Dell made a strategic move to adapt to the growing demand for high-performance gaming systems by acquiring Alienware, the renowned brand known for its specialized gaming computers. This decision allowed Dell to tap further into market of gaming enthusiasts and add to their products by using Alienware's expertise in crafting cutting-edge gaming PCs to aid the XPS line of Dell computers. Before the acquisition, Alienware had already established itself as a leader in the gaming PC industry, known for its distinctive designs and powerful hardware configurations. Dell recognized the opportunity to enhance its product portfolio and cater to a specific demographic that prioritized gaming performance. By integrating Alienware into its operations, Dell gained a significant foothold in the gaming market. Alienware retained its brand identity and continued to produce high-end gaming systems while also benefiting from Dell's manufacturing and distribution capabilities.
