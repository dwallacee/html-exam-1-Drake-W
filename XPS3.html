Dell revamps the XPS line to compete with Alienware, eventually buys alienware in 2006

Source: Wikipedia. Dell XPS. 2023. wikipedia.org (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dell_XPS#XPS_10)

In 2005, the XPS line of computers was revamped to compete with rival company Alienware. The company had considered purchasing the rival since 2005, but eventually did so on March 22, 2006.
