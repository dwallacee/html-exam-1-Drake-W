The XPS 10, a tablet computer, is unveiled in 2012, but dicontinued in 2013

Source: Wikipedia. Dell XPS. 2023. wikipedia.org (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dell_XPS#XPS_10)

The Dell XPS 10, released in 2012, stands as a Windows based tablet within Dell's XPS lineup. With a 10.1-inch display, it was a compact and portable design. Powered by a Qualcomm Snapdragon S4 dual-core processor, the tablet was created with a balance between performance and energy efficiency in mind. Operating on Windows RT, it offered a productivity-oriented program, supporting Microsoft Office and other tools from the Windows Store. The XPS 10 catered to consumers who were seeking a lightweight and versatile device for working on the go, combining a sleek looking table with the capabilities of Windows programming. While technological advancements have happened since its creation, the XPS 10 played a significant role in Dells entrance into the tablet market, which it is still apart of to this day.
